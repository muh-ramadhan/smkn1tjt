Form Wizard

A great way to provide a multi-step form for your customers to fill out. These support any component, and can also be programatically triggered.

Tap to Open Form Wizard
What effects can Form Wizards have?

Form wizards can use the same effect as our menu system. Meaning, you can have them appear from the left, right, top, bottom or even modals.

What content can I use in Form Wizards?

Any element from Dalonet will work inside of a Form Wizard, so you can create rich experiences for your users.

Can I trigger programatically?

As in the case of the menus, yes, of course! You can even change their size so they're not full screen if you wish.
